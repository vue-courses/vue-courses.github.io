Analytics and Data Collection

This website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymized user behavior data data, such as geographic location, browser, and operating system. When Vue.js Courses starts to earn some money, I'll transition to a more ethical analytics service like Simple Analytics.

Personal Data

Vue.js Courses allows users to submit their email address, which are saved to a database. Your email address is never shared with anyone. I only send emails relevant to what you subscribed for, and as infrequently as possible. If you would like your email address removed fom the database, get in touch and I will remove it.
